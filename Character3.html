Aviva

Aviva is a member and the third-in-command of the Wild Kratts crew.
She is a biomechanical engineer and the creator of all the Wild Kratts's
inventions, most notably the Tortuga, Creature Power Suits, and the Creature Pods.
Aviva is a strong leader and an active member of the crew. However, she can
take competitions a little too seriously, and can oftentimes be quite stubborn.
While she has been shown to be somewhat arrogant in her abilities
(no doubt due to her intelligence), she does care about the well being
of her crew and the animals they encounter, and she does not value her
inventions over her crewmates. Although often hard at work, she can
sometimes be lazy and feel unmotivated.
