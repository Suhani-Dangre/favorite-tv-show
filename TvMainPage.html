The Umbrella Academy

On october 1st, 43 kids were randomly born and gifted with strange powers. A man named Regginal Hargreeves adopted 7 of these kids. After a couple years, Mr. Hargreeves passes away causing the siblings to get together again. By this time, there is 5 of them left. Number 1, Luther, was living on the moon for a project his father had given him. Number 2, Diego, got kicked out of the police academy but still continued to fight crime with a stolen police walkie-talkie. Number 3, Allison, is a movie star and has a kid. Number 4, Klaus, no one knows where he is. Number 5, Five, is lost in time. Number 6, Vanya, wrote a book on all the family secrets and is now mostly hated by the whole family Number 7, Ben, (diseased) lives in klaus's mind (klaus can summon the dead).
